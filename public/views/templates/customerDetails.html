Reservation Policy

Refunds: In the event of a cancellation, store credit or a rain check is offered if something beyond your control comes up. Worst case is a $25 dollar cancellation fee per bike. (Refunds are NOT given in the rare event that it rains- we can’t control the weather!)

Rental Day: One rental day is defined as Open-Close of that day's store hours (Please refer to angrycatfishbicycle.com for current store hours). Rentals are not 24 hours. We need to have these bikes back before we close so that we can prepare them for more riders the next day.

Damage: Off-road riding can be unpredictable. There are rocks, roots, bridges, mud, and the occasional tabletop to get rad on. Sometimes people fall, crash, or fall into creeks. Whatever the situation, you are responsible for damage to our bike’s. You will be given a chance to ride and test your bike before your ride. ALL bikes are washed, tuned and inspected after every rental. We take good care of our bikes and are proud of how well they look.
